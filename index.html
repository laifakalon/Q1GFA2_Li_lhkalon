Norie Coffee's Beginning

Norie Coffee is a specialty single origin coffee shop at Matina, Davao City. We bring coffee back to its basics, brewing with only the best ingredients to prove that less is better.

Whether you like your coffee bold and black, white with cream, or rich brown with dark chocolate, we have you covered with our Origins line. React to support the coffee you like best!
